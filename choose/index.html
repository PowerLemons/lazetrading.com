EBYSU

EBYSU is a dietary supplement brand based in Vancouver. They offer a wide array of different supplements, such as L-Glutamine, Milk Thistle, Apple Cider Vinegar, and Ashwagandha. They sell on both Amazon and their own brand’s website in the United States and Canada
EBYSU came to us for help with improving the Amazon SEO of their products because of our up-to-date knowledge of ranking on the search results of keywords.
There are 2 parts to a successful Amazon SEO strategy: keyword research and keyword ranking.

KEYWORD RESEARCH

Before we can go about ranking the search results of any keywords, we need to know what should be those keywords that we’re ranking on!
For example, let’s look at Alibaba. In order for a manufacturer to be seen on Alibaba, they need to show up where potential business clients are looking: the search results. If business clients can’t find a manufacturer in these search results, there’s no reason why anyone would even know that the manufacturer exists.
This same concept goes for Amazon. Your products need to show up where the customers are looking: the search results!
It’s our job to do our research to find out where customers are looking so we can convert them into buyers. Furthermore, we also need to identify the keywords that we stand a chance at competing in against the competition - not all keywords are made equally
Using our knowledge of keyword research, we identified the keywords that gave the best chance at competing against the competition and making the sale. Factors include how much market share the competition has in certain keywords, and the review count of the competition.

KEYWORD RANKING

Following keyword research, we moved on to actually ranking EBYSU’s products on the search results of the keywords that we identified.
The method of ranking that we chose was giveaways. Amazon SEO relies on sales; the more sales a product does, the better its ranking is, while the lower a product does in its sales, the worse its ranking becomes. Through giveaways, we’re able to drive enough sales to EBYSU’s products to better their keyword ranking and, therefore, their sales (due to them improving in visibility to the consumer).
Using a system that we built that utilized Facebook Ads and Facebook Messenger, we ranked EBYSU’s products on these keywords through giveaways. We drove deal claimers through Facebook Ads to Facebok Messenger, where our automated messaging system took over and coordinate the giveaway on our behalf with minimal need for actual human labor.
Through this system, we ensured that enough product was given away for a certain amount of days in order to rank on the keywords.
Following the conclusion of the giveaway, we successfully ranked on all of the keywords.

CONCLUSION

Amazon SEO is absolutely crucial to generating sales on Amazon’s marketplace as it drives customer visibility.
Using our knowledge of keyword research, we identified the best possible keywords for generating sales. Then, with our proven keyword ranking system, we successfully ranked on the search results of these keywords.
EBYSU recently sold in late-2019 for $1M. We’re proud to have played a part in their success and buyout!

Gnawrishing

Started in 2017, Gnawrishing is a brand that offers sensory tools to children and adults with autism, ADHD, and oral motor challenges.
We brought the sales of the brand to the point that it made an average of $15k-$20k per month in revenue at a 30% profit margin (so, $5k-$6k a month in profit) over a 2 year period.
We coordinated all steps of the process of selling on Amazon, from product conceptualization to sourcing to Amazon listing optimization and SEO.

PRODUCT CONCEPTUALIZATION

On Amazon, we noticed that a popular product being sold that would fit this brand was a silicone teether in the style of a toy brick that children and adults would chew on to curb their stress.
Based on our criteria for validating products, we identified that this product was an exceptional product for garnering sales, and we decided to work on launching this product.
Later on, after we found success with the 1st product which generated $15k a month in revenue, we capitalized on this product by releasing silicone teethers in other popular designs which brought in an additional $5k a month in revenue.

AMAZON SEO

Excelling at Amazon SEO is absolutely crucial as this is a major way for buyers to find your product. If your product doesn’t have good Amazon SEO, most of your buyers will not be able to find your product at all. Hence why we put great importance in killing it with Amazon SEO.
The goal of Amazon SEO is to rank your product listing to a spot in which buyers are most likely to buy the product. It is nearly always the 1st page in the search results, and it is often high up on the search results on Page 1 (for example, Top 5 or Top 10).
Using our proprietary software, we identified keywords to rank on on Amazon in order to get the best shot at garnering sales.
With our Amazon SEO ranking tactics, we ranked to the top of Page 1 on all of Gnawrishing’s products. Shortly after, sales jumped incrementally each day until their peak (for example, $15k a month in revenue for the brand’s 1st product).

AMAZON LISTING OPTIMIZATION

There are three main components to an Amazon listing: the images, the copywriting, and the price.
Copywriting is crucial not only because it helps convince the buyer to buy the product, but also plays a role in Amazon determining the SEO of your product listing. The more optimized your listing’s copywriting is, the easier it is to excel at Amazon SEO and rank. Using our proprietary software, we determined important keywords to include in Gnawrishing’s copywriting, and then wrote the copywriting based on the customer’s reviews with a priority of catering to the customer’s emotions & desires.
Images are the most important part of your listing - humans are visual beings - we mainly buy based on what we see, and not what we read. These images are infographics that are based on your copywriting and show your product’s photography. Like with the copywriting, these images cater to the customer’s emotions and desires for the product - for example, we show use cases for the product which helps the customer put themselves into the customers shoes. We created Gnawrishing’s images based on these concepts to create buyer-ready images.
When it comes to price, customers on Amazon are price sensitive, and sales can change drastically based on the price of the product. Since Amazon SEO is dependent on sales, and Amazon SEO is crucial to garnering sales, it was important to constantly set the price in a way in which we maintain our keyword ranking while maximizing profits.

CONCLUSION

Successfully selling on Amazon is an intricate process which can fail and result in a huge loss of money if any of its crucial necessities aren’t done right.
We nailed the most major points to an Amazon listing for Gnawrishing. Product conceptualization is crucial because if the wrong product is sold, there will be no demand and no sales will be made. Amazon SEO is crucial because if people can’t find your product, how can they buy it? Amazon listing optimization is crucial because without an amazing set of images and copywriting, and proper management of your product’s price, your product will fall short in attaining and convincing buyers to buy your product.
Furthermore, there are many other small yet important factors which have to be nailed right in order to eliminate unnecessary financial losses and wasted time. For example, shipping with the right freight forwarder, meeting Amazon prep requirements, and the many quirks that Amazon has with their Seller Central dashboard (i.e the website where your business’s Amazon presence is managed).
